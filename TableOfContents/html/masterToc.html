INSTRUCTIONS FOR EVERYONE:

GETTING STARTED
Click on the etherpad link for your Track, Practice Space or Network Gathering
  • Click on the green box in the upper right corner and enter your name
  • Click on the link for a session and read it. DO NOT COMMENT ON THE SESSION'S ETHERPAD.
  • Return to your TPSNG’s etherpad and leave comments and questions about each proposal underneath the link to the session in the chat box. We will use those notes in finalizing selections. 
  • If a proposal seems like it is a better fit for other TPSNGs, email them the relevant links. (Here is the contact form for all AMC2016 TPSNGs: https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1Ou4WOq3aWTqfjPYT_N0HxbhTvfDVQNn90MWq_2nTuhc/edit#gid=0)
  • Schedule a call, Skype or Google Hangout with your coordinating team to discuss your session selections. Schedule this early, because you may need to hold an additional call. If you would like Morgan on the call, email her at morgan@alliedmedia.org.
  • To submit: see specific instructions at the top of your TPSNG’s etherpad.
  • The deadline to submit your recommendations is March 27th.
  • After we review all recommendations, we will contact you with final selections and invite feedback. Your recommendations are the most critical part of our review process.

SESSION REVIEW CRITERIA
Please review this criteria before making your recommendations. The AMC is looking for sessions that:
  • Have a clear connection to media-based organizing (https://www.alliedmedia.org/media-based-organizing)
  • Are accessible (https://drive.google.com/folderview?id=0B7uAYw8j8fREcXo3MzRlRXlZREE&usp=sharing)
  • Align with AMP’s Network Principles (https://alliedmedia.org/about/network-principles)
  • Advance the visions of the 2016 Tracks, Practice Spaces and Network Gatherings (https://www.alliedmedia.org/tracks-practice-spaces-network-gatherings)
  • Meet the additional criteria of What makes a good session (scroll to bottom of page) (https://www.alliedmedia.org/news/propose-session-amc2016)
